What Is Q-SpecTrum?

Not software. A new way to work with AI.

Not Software

Q-SpecTrum is a thinking framework, not an application. It's a structured set of files that transforms any AI model into a complete company operating system with 15 specialized roles.

🤖
Any AI Model

Works with Claude, GPT-4, Gemini, LLaMA, and more. Upload the files, speak the activation phrase, and the AI becomes your company. Model-agnostic by design.

Shared Brain

AI + Folder = Intelligence. The folder is the shared memory. Every conversation, decision, and output is stored structurally. Your company grows smarter over time.
